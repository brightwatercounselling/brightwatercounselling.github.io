About Me

I have spent most of my working life in dentistry as a professional Dental Hygienist
with a particular interest in working with nervous people and those experiencing dental phobias.
After a difficult and traumatic period in my life,
I sought help from a therapist - an experience which changed my life
and I decided to re-train and change career.

I am now a qualified, fully insured psychotherapeutic counsellor,
and an accredited member of the National Counselling Society and ACCPH.
I follow the NCS ethical framework for good practice.
I studied Humanistic Psychotherapy at Metanoia Institute for two years
then completed my Diploma with Mercure Counselling and Training alliance.
I am also a qualified as a Mental Health First-Aider.

I gained my clinical experience working as a counsellor with
Buckinghamshire Mind and Clean Slate - a charity based in Oxfordshire
who work to support survivors of abuse. Previous to that I had a clinical placement
