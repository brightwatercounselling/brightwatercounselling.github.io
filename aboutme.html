About Me

I have spent most of my working life in dentistry as a professional Dental Hygienist
with a particular interest in working with nervous people and those experiencing dental phobias.
A few years ago I went through a difficult and traumatic period in my life,
this led me to seek help from a therapist - an experience which changed my life
and I decided to re-train and change career.

I am now a qualified, fully insured counsellor and an accredited member of the ACCPH,
I follow the BACP ethical framework for good practice.
I studied Humanistic Psychotherapy at Metanoia Institute in London for two years
then completed a course in counselling and psychotherapy near Oxford;
I am continuing to study including continuing professional development (CPD)
and last year I qualified as a Mental Health First-Aider.

I gained my clinical experience working as a volunteeer counsellor with
Buckinghamshire Mind and Clean Slate - a charity based in Oxfordshire
who work to support victims of abuse. Previous to that I had a clinical placement
